Echo Bloom

A simple click-based sound prototype. Click each small image button to turn a sound layer on or off.

No sound layers active.

Concept

Echo Bloom uses click as the main interaction. The user clicks small image buttons to activate or deactivate different sound layers. The calm colours, round shapes and soft visual feedback match the relaxation context.
